Welcome to Committed.

I've been working with Linux and other open-source / Free software since 1997. I've built a career around navigating and advising about these software projects, and yet I have always been somewhat...shy...about making these experiences public. Thus, my various employers have benefited from the experience, but I have not been giving back nearly as much to the community as I have gained from it.

That needs to change.

As an engineering-minded individual, solving problems is basically my thing, man, so what better way to solve this problem than by setting up a space to share my experiences?

And here we are. My first steps on this journey center around GStreamer and Python. Head over and take a look at the work in progress.
